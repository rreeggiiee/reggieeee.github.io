In Limbus Company, "Crushers" are a formidable class of enemies known for complex attacks and status effects that winrate unable to count and using it is useless against them.

Ricardo-Middle Big Brother

Ricardo cannot be defeated. To win the fight, one simply needs to bring his health to half, or last 2 skill rotations after 3 turns, then a cutscene will happen in which the player will be able to proceed after. The fight is uniquely scripted so that Ishmael cannot die. You can exploit this heavily. Keep your team small: Do not bring a full team of 6. Bring Ishmael + 1 or 2 powerful Blunt-damage Sinners. Having fewer Sinners gives your remaining characters extra skill slots, allowing them to overpower Ricardo's clashes. Redirect attacks: Use Ishmael to tank hits and redirect attacks away from your damage dealers.Unopposed dogpiling: Ricardo has certain turns where his skill slots reach 1 speed. Do not challenge these clashes. Instead, use unopposed attacks with your DPS to burst down his HP and trigger his stagger thresholds. Skills
